What motivates Us?

We started yesterday! haha we like to keep things exciting, and that includes our history. We may joke around and say we started yesterday – but the truth is, we’ve been riding the waves for years! Born out of a shared love for water and adventure, our founders took one wild rafting trip and decided the world needed more of this. So, they grabbed their paddles, and with a few enthusiastic friends, set out to bring that same thrill to everyone.

Of course, we’ve come a long way since then, but the spirit has stayed the same: to give you a heart-pumping, laughter-filled experience on the water. Every rapid we conquer and every calm moment we enjoy is a reminder of how far we’ve come (even if it feels like it was just yesterday). So, whether you’re a seasoned adventurer or a first-time floater, we’re here to make sure your journey is unforgettable – because hey, it all started with a good laugh and a splash of water!
